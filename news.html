FAQ / Support

Find answers about joining, operations, activity, events, and support channels.

How do I join?

Complete the application form on the Join page. Applications are reviewed through a mix of automatic and manual approval steps.

What is the activity rule?

Every pilot must complete at least one flight every two weeks. Inactivity can affect your rank progression.

What does FlightLinq do?

FlightLinq handles flight tracking, scoring, rank calculation, statistics, and event creation/evaluation.

How is support handled?

Support is available through the website ticket flow and Discord, which is also used for community communication and events.
