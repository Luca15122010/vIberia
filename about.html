About vIberia

vIberia is built to feel like a real airline website while operating as a Virtual Airline behind the scenes.

Vision

We focus on realistic operations, a high-end presentation, and a structured pilot experience with clear standards and a professional atmosphere.

FlightLinq Role

FlightLinq handles flight tracking, rank progression, statistics, activity checks, and event processing. The website itself does not store pilot accounts or logins.

Activity Policy

Pilots must complete at least one flight every two weeks. Inactivity can affect rank status and operational standing.

Community

Discord supports communication, events, and help, while FlightLinq remains the primary system for operations.
